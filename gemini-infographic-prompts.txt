================================================================================
GLOBAL STYLE GUIDE (apply to every image)
================================================================================

GENRE & FORMAT
  Vertical infographic comic page — one self-contained story moment per image.
  Reads top-to-bottom like a graphic novel page or editorial infographic strip.
  Professional business biography aesthetic — NOT childish cartoon, NOT anime,
  NOT Marvel/superhero, NOT 3D Pixar.

ART STYLE
  • Clean vector-infographic meets editorial comic line art
  • Consistent medium-weight ink outlines (1–2 px feel), flat color fills with subtle gradients only
  • Limited shading: soft cel-shading or none — clarity over realism
  • Geometric simplification of architecture, devices, and icons
  • Optional: very subtle paper texture overlay (5% opacity) for storybook warmth
  • Panels may be separated by thin gold rules or cream gutters

COLOR PALETTE (strict — match website brand)
  • Background / dominant dark: Deep navy #0C1222
  • Secondary panels / cards: Slate blue #151D32
  • Primary text & highlights: Warm cream #F4F0E8
  • Accent / emphasis / borders: Antique gold #C9A227
  • Soft accent highlights: Pale gold #E8D48A
  • Muted secondary text: Cool gray #8B95A8
  • Healthcare accent (sparingly): Soft teal #4A9B9B at 40% opacity
  • Life sciences accent (sparingly): Soft violet #7B6BA8 at 40% opacity
  • Avoid: neon colors, pure white backgrounds, rainbow gradients, red-heavy palettes

TYPOGRAPHY & TEXT ON IMAGE
  • Maximum 1 title plate + 1 subtitle + optional 3–5 keyword chips per image
  • Title plate: serif feel (Instrument Serif style) — cream text on navy or gold bar
  • Subtitle / dates: small caps sans-serif (DM Sans style) — muted cream or gold
  • Keyword chips: rounded pills, navy fill, gold border, cream micro-text
  • NO speech bubbles unless specified; prefer caption plates at top or bottom
  • All text must be spelled correctly, high contrast, readable at mobile width

COMPOSITION (vertical 9:16)
  • Top 15%: chapter label + title plate (reserved band)
  • Middle 55%: main illustration / infographic storytelling
  • Bottom 20%: 3–5 icon chips OR stat callouts OR scene caption
  • Safe margins: 5% padding on all sides — no critical detail at edges
  • Visual flow: guide eye top → center → bottom (Z or single focal path)

MOOD & TONE
  • Calm authority, craftsmanship, optimism without hype
  • Enterprise credibility suitable for hiring executives reviewing a resume site
  • Human warmth — real workplaces, not sterile stock-photo poses

NEGATIVE PROMPT (append to every generation if Gemini supports it)
  blurry, low resolution, photorealistic faces, uncanny valley, 3D render, anime,
  chibi, clipart, watermark, logo soup, trademark logos, messy text, misspelled words,
  speech bubble overload, violent imagery, dark horror tone, neon cyberpunk,
  crowded composition, horizontal landscape layout, white background,
  clean-shaven face, no mustache, beard without mustache, inconsistent facial hair

CHARACTER BIBLE — PROTAGONIST (use consistently in all panels)
================================================================================

NAME IN PROMPTS: "The Protagonist" (representing Prem — technology leader)

APPEARANCE
  • South Asian professional male, age progresses slightly across chapters:
    - Cognizant 2003–2005: mid-20s, eager focus
    - Cognizant 2006–2017: late 20s to mid-30s, confident, professional
    - Cognizant 2018–2021: late 30s to early 40s, seasoned leader
    - EC Group 2021–present: early-to-mid 40s, approachable executive-builder
  • Mustache: REQUIRED in every panel — consistent defining feature across all ages.
    - Well-groomed, medium thickness, dark brown/black matching hair
    - 2003–2005: slightly thinner/neat chevron or pencil mustache (younger but still visible)
    - 2006 onward: fuller, mature mustache — same style throughout for recognition
    - Never clean-shaven; never exaggerated handlebar unless specified
  • Hair: short, neat, dark brown/black
  • Attire evolves:
    - Early mainframe era: business casual shirt, ID lanyard, no tie
    - Hartford / leadership: button-down or blazer, sometimes rolled sleeves
    - Architect / PM era: smart casual blazer, open collar
    - Startup / Strategy.ai: modern tech leader — dark navy or charcoal shirt, no tie, optional light jacket
  • Expression: thoughtful, focused, collaborative — never arrogant or exaggerated
  • Body language: leads from front — standing beside team, pointing at boards, typing at desk — not towering over others

CONSISTENCY RULES
  • Same face structure across all images (simplified comic style — not photo portrait)
  • Mustache must appear in every image where the protagonist's face is visible — even in profile or 3/4 view
  • Same color palette on character clothing: navy, cream, subtle gold accents
  • When shown in silhouette for scale scenes, maintain recognizable hair/profile outline with distinct mustache shape on profile
