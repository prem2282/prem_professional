PROLOGUE — A NEW KIND OF ASCENT (APRIL 2021)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In April 2021, I made a deliberate choice: leave the comfort of a global
delivery machine and step into the volatility of product creation.

EC Group Datasoft assigned me to Strategy.ai — then known as metaphor.com, a
startup with ambition, a blank architecture slate, and the need for someone who
could be Solution Architect, product development lead, and culture setter in the
same breath.

I did not join to oversee from a distance. I joined to build — team, pipeline,
platform, and philosophy.

The keywords of this chapter would become: advanced RAG pipelines · LLMs ·
Knowledge Graph · HIPAA/GDPR compliance · Security · Context-aware systems ·
Workflow builders · Cloud infrastructure.

But before any of that vocabulary existed in our pitch decks, there was an empty
repository and a question: *What does world-class product engineering look like
when you are starting from zero?*


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — FROM ZERO TO SAAS (2021–2023)
metaphor.com → Strategy.ai · Foundation Years
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**The mandate**
Join as Solution Architect and product development lead. Stand up a team from
scratch. Deliver an enterprise SaaS product for vision and strategy management —
secure, scalable, and built to earn the trust of large organizations.

**The philosophy**
We did not inherit a mature engineering culture — we authored one.

Extreme Programming. Test-Driven Development. Every engineer exposed to the full
lifecycle — from inception through deployment — so that ownership was not a
poster on the wall but a daily habit. I believe individuals play to their
strengths when they understand the whole system; we built that understanding
deliberately.

**The platform**
From bare metal of ideas to running cloud:

  • Cloud architecture designed and implemented from the ground up
  • Clean, secure CI/CD pipelines — automation as a first-class product feature
  • Scalable multi-tenant SaaS patterns for enterprise strategy and vision management
  • API-first design: REST, WebSocket, integration-ready surfaces
  • Engineering stack: Python · Django · JavaScript · Vue.js · Quasar · React · PostgreSQL · MongoDB

AWS became the workshop: IAM, EC2, S3, Lambda, SQS, API Gateway, CloudWatch,
CloudTrail, Route 53, RDS, and the operational discipline of observability, cost
awareness, and secure deployment.

**The outcome culture**
Agile delivery matured. Reusable architecture emerged. Governance was light but
real. Strong ownership replaced hero culture.

This was the period that proved I could compress what Cognizant taught across
decades into a startup clock — without sacrificing quality.

SECTION 2 — THE GENAI HORIZON (2023–2026)
Strategy.ai · Intelligence at the Core
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Then the world changed — again — and so did we.

As large language models moved from novelty to infrastructure, Strategy.ai
expanded its product catalog into a GenAI-powered suite. We rebranded with
conviction: the metaphor had become the method — **Strategy.ai**.

**The product vision**
Help organizations align business strategy with intelligence that is accurate,
precise, and focused — not generic chat for its own sake, but governed knowledge,
workflow, and decision support embedded in how leaders actually work.

**What we built**
  • Enterprise AI copilots and agents for strategy execution
  • Governed knowledge workflows with retrieval you can trust
  • Proprietary-content chatbots — answers grounded in *your* data, not the public internet
  • Advanced RAG pipelines · vector databases · context engineering
  • Knowledge Graph integrations for connected enterprise truth
  • Workflow builders for domain-specific AI mentors and coaches
  • AI assistants spanning use cases from leadership alignment to operational guidance

**The model layer**
R&D and production adoption across the modern AI stack:

  OpenAI · Anthropic · Google Gemini · Meta Llama · AWS Bedrock
  LangChain · agentic workflows · enterprise chat · AI governance & guardrails

**The trust layer**
For enterprise clients — especially in regulated domains — security was not a
feature backlog item; it was the price of admission:

  HIPAA · GDPR · secure SaaS deployment · compliance-by-design onboarding

**Measured impact**
  • 40% faster task turnaround
  • 30% improved collaboration
  • 50% reduction in deployment cycles

**Leadership scope today**
As Senior Director of Technology, I own technology strategy, platform roadmap,
and engineering for Strategy.ai's enterprise AI SaaS — partnering with founders
and product leaders to turn market opportunity into architecture, and architecture
into shipped capability.

SECTION 4 — LEADERSHIP PHILOSOPHY AT EC GROUP
How We Work · How I Lead
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**On people**
I lead from the front — not manage from the top. I trust the team, grow with the
team, learn from the team, and guide by example. Transparency is not a value
statement; it is how we run sprint reviews, incident calls, and roadmap debates.

**On craft**
I am still a coder. Product leadership without implementation empathy is
theory. TDD and XP are not nostalgia — they are how we keep velocity without
trading away tomorrow's maintainability.

**On learning**
Technology never stops; neither do I. The same curiosity that pulled me from
COBOL to LLMs pulls me into the next framework, the next model, the next failure
that teaches more than the last success.

**On teaching**
Knowledge grows when shared. Mentoring, academy contributions, and guest lectures
are not side quests — they are how ecosystems get stronger.

**On the mission**
Strategy.ai exists so organizations can execute strategy with clarity — and now,
with intelligence that respects their data, their compliance boundaries, and their
ambition.

Partnering with founders means translating vision into roadmaps, roadmaps into
architecture, and architecture into outcomes you can measure.

Cognizant taught me enterprise gravity.

The ascent continues. The canvas is larger than ever.
